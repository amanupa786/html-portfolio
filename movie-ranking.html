The Best Movies According to Aman

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Big Short

A gripping portrayal of the 2008 financial crisis, following a group of investors who profited by predicting and betting against the housing market collapse.

Moneyball

The true story of Billy Beane, who used data analytics to build a competitive baseball team on a limited budget, revolutionizing sports management.

Billions

A drama series exploring the intense rivalry between a hedge fund manager and a U.S. Attorney, set in the high-stakes world of finance and power.
